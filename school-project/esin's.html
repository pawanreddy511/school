         ABOUT

Collabrate,create,compile is the mantra that this group is founded upon. This group's motto is to inculcate skill of team work rather than working individually. This club includes group based on the stream of individual's interest like A.I, gaming, web development, IOT, graphics, coding. It is basically an outcome of the creative minds who have put in alot of hardwork and effort.

It also aims to create an atmosphere of passion for the advancing technologies. The foundation stone of the club is tram work.Team work as we know is a very important quality in professionals and surely results in success. Team work acts as a medium for sharing our knowledge and exchanging views. This club is also a means of experience for students preparing them for their future.
